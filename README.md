EcoLens is an AI-powered research and compliance assistant that transforms sustainability reports, agricultural waste data, and industrial byproducts into actionable circular economy insights. 
It allows users to upload documents such as sustainability reports or waste material datasheets and automatically analyzes them through a multi-agent AI workflow.


EcoLens uses a 3-layer AI agent system: 
Insight Lens - Extracts and understands material composition from uploaded documents. 
Opportunity Lens - Maps waste materials to potential sustainable and commercial applications. 
Compliance Lens - Evaluates outputs against environmental regulations and flags risks or missing permits.

EcoLens acts like a digital sustainability consultant that: 


📄 Extracts key material and environmental data from documents 

🔍 Identifies valuable resources hidden in waste streams 

♻️ Suggests circular economy applications (e.g., biochar, compost, packaging materials) 

⚖️ Checks compliance against environmental and regulatory frameworks 

📊 Generates structured reports and readiness scores

🌱 Interactive Concept Explorer - The Interactive Concept Explorer acts as an AI-powered guide through complex sustainability, compliance, and circular economy documents. Instead of manually searching through hundreds of pages, users can explore an automatically generated hierarchy of key concepts such as biochar production, composting, recycling targets, hazardous waste management, and resource recovery. Each concept can be expanded to reveal subtopics, explanations, and related themes, enabling users to quickly understand unfamiliar domains, discover important ideas, and navigate large collections of documents in an intuitive way


📑 AI Slide Deck Generator - The AI Slide Deck Generator automatically converts uploaded reports, research papers, policy documents, and sustainability assessments into professional presentation decks within seconds. Instead of manually creating slides, users can generate a structured presentation with key findings, executive summaries, data-driven insights, recommendations, and visual highlights extracted directly from the source material. 


📄 AI Research Brief - The AI Research Brief automatically synthesizes large volumes of information into a concise, structured report. It extracts the most important findings, identifies recurring themes, highlights challenges and opportunities, and generates executive-level summaries tailored for decision-makers. This allows researchers, policymakers, and sustainability professionals to rapidly understand key insights without spending hours reviewing lengthy reports, making information more accessible and actionable.


🕸️ Knowledge Map - The Knowledge Map transforms unstructured document content into an interactive network of connected ideas. It visualizes relationships between waste streams, recycling processes, products, regulations, policies, and stakeholders, helping users see how different concepts influence one another. By revealing hidden connections across multiple documents, the map supports systems thinking, accelerates research, and helps users identify innovation opportunities, dependencies, and potential areas for intervention that might otherwise remain unnoticed.


📊 Policy Impact Simulator The Policy Impact Simulator helps users evaluate the potential consequences of proposed policies and regulatory changes before implementation. By analyzing factors such as landfill taxes, recycling mandates, reporting requirements, or sustainability incentives, the simulator estimates how organizations, industries, and environmental outcomes may be affected. It enables evidence-based decision-making by highlighting potential benefits, risks, costs, and unintended consequences, helping stakeholders design more effective and sustainable policies.
